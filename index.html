Community-based immigration services

C-connect is a One-stop community base referral service that promotes inclusion, education, independence, community integration, career coaching and resources to New Immigrants and their families in need.

C-connect is an organization that works with individuals to develop empowerment programs that focus on Youth Development and building solid foundations with new immigrants and their families to bring a change to our communities.

We're Here
To Help

We understand all your immigration concerns and we are here to hold your hand through the process.
From your application to community integration, we are here to help.

We've created a community-based platform to make the immigration process as seamless as possible for you and your family.

As a community, we recognize the need to band together as immigrants. We know first hand how tedious the process can be without the proper guidance and assistance so we created this platform as a go-to network for everything you might need.

We are located in 9520 Berger Road, Suite 212, Columbia, MD 21046, and we offer consultation services, referrals, and assistance where we can.
You are more than welcome to reach out to us at any point of your immigration journey. We're always happy to help.

Our Core Value

  • Driven by compassion, we treat all people with dignity and respect.
  • We commit ourselves to build trusting, respectful relationships with clients and partners.
  • Dedicated to service excellence, we direct our resources to those most vulnerable.
  • We foster open communication and accountability through our actions.
  • We embrace community collaborations to expand our capacity and effectiveness.

Nearly every aspect of life is more complicated for immigrant families. Whether it is getting into college, finding employment, or accessing health care, there are hurdles that make life more difficult for immigrants. The bureaucratic aspects of the immigration process are incredibly complex and constantly changing. It is enormously rewarding to assist people through the legal process.
