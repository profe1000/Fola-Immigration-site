Our Program

Immigration laws and regulations can seem complicated. We are here to help you and your family understand your options and navigate the process. If you do not speak English, you'll be matched with a professional, caring interpreter who can help translate documents or communicate with your immigration officer.

Our Experience: For more than 20 years, Cconnet has provided quality legal services to immigrants with limited resources. We have served individuals from more than 90 countries and have been a Department of Justice accredited provider of immigration legal services since 2000. We offer affordable (low bono), no-charge (pro bono) and sliding scale services, based on client resources.
