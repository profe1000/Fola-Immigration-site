Employment Services

Employment referral
Community Connect (C-connect), formally know as CC Nanny Placement Agency, has been matching high-quality nannies with families since 2012. We continue to give you peace of mind in the comfort of your home. Our Nannies are carefully selected candidates with prior experience and/or formal child growth and development training. Our nannies come from every part of the world. Our Parents and families are professionals in different careers. 

We offer a broad range of services: live-in and live-out, short & long-term temporary, summer, occasional evening, and weekend. We are also concerned with your children’s physical, emotional, intellectual, and recreational needs.

Making A Smart Choice in childcare is one of the most important decisions you will ever make. C-connect Nanny Placement is licensed and bounded in Maryland and is your resource for in-home nannies.

If you are seeking quality in-home childcare assistance or looking for someone to help you organize your home, please give us a call at 443-763-1729 to discuss your needs and schedule a free consultation in our office or the comfort of your home. If you are seeking work, please click On-Line Jobs above and follow the instructions. 
C-connect Nanny Placement Services is committed to providing personal service based on your specified needs. Our professional screening process significantly increases your ability to make the smart choice with your nanny selection.
At C-connect Nanny agency, we build excellent connections between our nannies and families. Our general Screening Process will ensure that you will perfectly match the family and the nanny with an exceptional assistant that will enhance your quality of life.
