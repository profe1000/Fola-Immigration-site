Immigration Assistance

At C-connect we provide our services through case management practice. We are not a law firm, and our consultations are not a substitute for the advice of a lawyer and do not contain or constitute legal advice. We are not affiliated with or sponsored by the United States government or any government agency. We work to provide advocacy and case management services for immigrants.
Case managers provide orientation within the legal system to undocumented immigrants, helping them fill out legal residency applications. Others help documented immigrants by planning work visas and citizenship tests, conducting home and work evaluation assessments, and providing access to social services.
